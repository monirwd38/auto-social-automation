The Problem

Consistent Personal Branding is Hard

As a full-stack developer, staying consistent on social media is time-consuming. Writing platform-optimized posts for LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and Facebook — twice a week — takes 3–5 hours minimum. It's either done inconsistently or not at all.

The goal was to build a system that handles everything — research, writing, visuals, and publishing — while still keeping a human in the loop for quality control.

"What if I could wake up Monday morning, get a Telegram message with a fully written post and AI-generated image, tap 'approve', and have it live on all 4 platforms before my first coffee?"

The Solution

A 10-Node Autonomous Workflow

Built entirely in n8n (self-hosted via Docker), the workflow runs on a schedule — no trigger needed. It researches trending topics, generates platform-specific content, creates visuals, and waits for your approval before posting.

How It Works

Step-by-Step Flow

1

Schedule Trigger

Fires automatically every Monday & Thursday at 9:00 AM (Asia/Dhaka timezone)

2

Web Research

Tavily API searches trending automation & business topics in real-time

3

AI Content Generation

GPT-4o generates all 4 platform posts + image prompt + reel script in one call

4

Image Generation

DALL·E 3 creates a branded visual from the AI-written image prompt

5

Telegram Preview

Full content package sent to Telegram for human review before posting

6

Wait for Approval

Workflow pauses. Reply "approve", "edit", or "reject" on Telegram

7

Publish to All Platforms

Buffer API posts simultaneously to LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram & Facebook

8

Log & Confirm

Google Sheets logs every post. Telegram confirms it's live.

Content Strategy

Built for Both Branding & Client Acquisition

The AI rotates content types strategically to maximize both audience growth and inbound client interest:

  • 40% Educational — Teach automation to non-tech people in plain language. Builds authority.
  • 30% Inspirational — Real-world results: time saved, revenue earned through automation. Builds trust.
  • 20% Case Study / Portfolio — Show workflows you've built. Directly attracts clients.
  • 10% Soft CTA — "Want this built for your business? DM me." Natural lead generation.
